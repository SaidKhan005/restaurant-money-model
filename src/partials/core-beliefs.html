🎯 Core Beliefs

Constraint Thinking Optimize the right constraint, or die optimizing the wrong one.
Results vs Levers Revenue is a result. Profit is a result. Cost is feedback, not strategy.
Sequence is Law Fix capacity before behavior. Fix behavior before economics. Fix economics before cost. Training can't fix broken structure.
Alignment Over Optimization Right energy direction beats high energy. Right capacity beats low labor %. Match systems to intended behavior, not benchmarks.
Cross-Layer Diagnosis Some problems span layers. "Rushed" is capacity overload showing up as experience failure. Trace root causes upstream.
